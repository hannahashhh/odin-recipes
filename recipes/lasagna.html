Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made of layered pasta sheets, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and melted cheese, typically baked until golden and bubbly.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or sausage
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Béchamel sauce or ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Italian herbs (basil, oregano)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, brown the ground beef or sausage with chopped onion and minced garlic.
  4. Add tomato sauce and Italian herbs to the meat mixture, simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese with beaten egg, salt, and pepper.
  6. Spread a layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  7. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, béchamel sauce (if using), and mozzarella cheese.
  8. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  9. Bake for 30-40 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  10. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
