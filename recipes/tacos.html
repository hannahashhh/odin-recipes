Tacos

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish consisting of a folded or rolled tortilla filled with various ingredients such as meat, beans, cheese, and vegetables, often topped with salsa, guacamole, or sour cream.

Ingredients

  • Taco shells (soft or hard)
  • Ground beef, chicken, or beans
  • Onion and garlic
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Lettuce
  • Shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
  • Salsa or pico de gallo
  • Guacamole or sour cream

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef or chicken in a skillet with chopped onion and minced garlic until browned.
  2. Season the meat with taco seasoning or spices of your choice.
  3. Warm the taco shells in the oven or microwave.
  4. Assemble the tacos by filling each shell with the cooked meat, chopped tomatoes, lettuce, and shredded cheese.
  5. Add salsa, guacamole, or sour cream as desired.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
